Privacy Policy

DTherapist ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients, including professional therapists ("Providers") and clients ("Clients"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information.

1. Data Collection

We may collect personal information such as name, email address, and age when users sign up for an account or interact with our platform. Additional data may include contact information, professional credentials, and profile details (bio, specialty, languages spoken).

2. Use of Information

The collected information helps us tailor support services and resources based on individual needs, verify provider credentials, and facilitate matches between providers and clients. We also use the data to monitor platform performance and send important notifications.

3. Data Security

Personal information is stored securely with access restricted to authorized personnel. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption and secure servers to prevent unauthorized access.

4. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their policies or handling of your information.

5. Cookies

Our site employs cookies for tracking purposes, enhancing user experiences. Users can disable cookies via browser settings if preferred.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to view, modify, or request deletion of their personal data shared on DTherapist.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Please review it regularly for any changes or additions.
