About DTherapist

"On that fateful day, my world came crashing down as I watched my 19-year-old son collapse in my presence while playing football. The shock, pain, and overwhelming grief that followed were indescribable. Losing him was like losing a part of myself, leaving an unfillable void in my life. As I navigated this isolating journey of bereavement, I realized I wasn’t alone—and so DTherapist was born."

On the first year remembrance, we received this message from Dr. Enesi Y. Salawu, his course adviser, department of mechanical engineering, Covenant University Ota;‘Dubbie was one of my best students. As his adviser, he was such a courageous, knowledgeable and passionate soul. Above all, always attentive and ready to achieve more in life. Sincerely speaking, his death made me calm to reflect more about life and loved my students more. He was supposed to be graduating now with this set. But his journey in life wasn't cut short but elevated to a place where he will enjoy peace round above. To the parents, friends and loved ones, we may be remembering him today crying, but remember that my own boy is rejoicing where he is because I surely know that he is in a very good place. God bless you all’.

DTherapist is dedicated to supporting individuals through mental health challenges, grief, and loss. Our platform provides compassionate, accessible, and professional mental health services for individuals of all ages, delivered in a safe, inclusive environment. We offer therapy, support groups, and a wealth of educational resources to guide you toward healing and growth.

Our Vision

"To become a beacon of hope, guiding individuals through mental health concerns, loss, and grief, fostering resilience and healing in our community."

Our Mission

"To provide compassionate support, resources, and guidance to those navigating mental health issues and grief, aiming to cultivate an environment where individuals find solace, renew their spirits, and find peace within themselves."

Our Objectives

  • Provide a compassionate, safe, and supportive community for individuals to share their experiences and emotions.
  • Offer resources and guidance for navigating mental health and grieving processes.
  • Facilitate connections with others who have experienced similar losses.
  • Provide access to expert advice, support groups, and therapy sessions.
  • Create a library of articles, videos, and podcasts on mental health, grief, healing, and wellness.
  • Offer personalized support plans and goal-setting to facilitate healing.
  • Partner with mental health professionals and organizations for additional support.
  • Establish a community forum for sharing stories, memories, and tributes.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Providing empathetic and caring support to all individuals seeking help.

Confidentiality

Ensuring a safe, secure space for open communication without fear of judgment.

Inclusivity

Respecting and honoring diverse backgrounds, including cultural and religious differences.

Authenticity

Fostering genuine connections and honest communication with all clients.

Collaboration

Working closely with clients and professionals to provide the best support possible.

What We Offer

DTherapist aims to create a compassionate, safe environment where you can thrive. Whether you seek professional counseling or a place to express yourself anonymously, we are here to support you.

Group Therapy

Supportive, group-based therapy for shared issues like anxiety, depression, and other mental health disorders.

Mental Health Counseling

Focuses on improving focus, relieving stress, and managing anxiety to support overall mental well-being.

Family Therapy

Helps families improve communication and resolve conflicts, often used to support children and adolescents.

Interpersonal Therapy (IPT)

Focused on resolving interpersonal issues, often used to treat depression and anxiety.
